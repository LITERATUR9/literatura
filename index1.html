LITERATUR9 Objectives
The LITERATUR9 plans to achieve the following:
- Provide Grade 9 RiScians access to an inclusive English literature website.

- A website offering verified and trustworthy information.
- Improve and develop the reading comprehension of Grade 9 RiScians.

- Create an online library that contains English literature for Grade 9 RiScians.
- Assisting teachers in providing students with materials that are both accessible and user-friendly.
About LITERATUR9
LITERATUR9—exploring the world of literature

The development of educational technology cleared the path for learning to be more convenient, accessible, and easy. It is an effective instrument that transforms education to engage students and support them in learning at a flexible and advanced level.

A website called LITERATUR9 promotes students, particularly those in Grade 9, to advance and deepen their understanding of the study of English literature.

The website is based on the Department of Education's book A Journey Through Anglo-American Literature.

It also discusses literature from the first to the fourth quarter, making the discussions of the stories more compelling and entertaining by including illustrations and interactive features. With LITERATUR9, exploring the world of literature is now at hand.

A Journey Through Anglo-American Literature

This course material is intended to help you create the cooperative, collaborative, and independent learning strategies for the target themes, concepts, and competencies that come after the development of your real-world, 21st-century skills.

The study was based on the textbook "A Journey Through Anglo-American Literature." The researchers used it as a guide for determining the proper and exact order of the literatures as well as the appropriateness of the information to employ. The literatures that can be read and seen on the website are all based on the Grade 9 English Most Essential Learning Competencies (MELCs), which will make the content and website itself as valid material to utilize.
